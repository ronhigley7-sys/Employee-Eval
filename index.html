Code of Conduct

Arnot Health Code of Conduct and Confidentiality Attestation

Handling Protected Health Information

For employees: Each Arnot Health employee shall attest and affirm the following:

  • I will comply with all applicable federal and state laws and regulations governing the institution and all policies of Arnot Health.

To maintain and foster this Code of Conduct, as an employee:

  • I will report any concern as soon as I become aware. If I do not feel comfortable reporting the concern to my direct manager, or do not feel the concern was adequately addressed, I will report the concern by means of the Compliance Hotline at ext. 4347 or 607-737-4347. I understand the Compliance Hotline number is listed on the intranet on the Corporate Compliance page or may be accessed via the Arnot Ogden Medical Center main hospital switchboard at 607-737-4100. I understand this concern may be an observation of a lack of compassion or a suspected violation of an organizational policy, federal regulation or law.
  • I will utilize other methods to communicate concerns if necessary. These include directly to the Compliance Officer, member of the Corporate Compliance Committee, President of Arnot Health, Audit Committee Chairperson or the NYS Department of Health. I am aware that a list of these contacts and numbers may be obtained from Health on Demand at ext. 4499 or 607-737-4499.

In regards to Confidentiality, I hereby acknowledge and attest to the following:

  • I received training regarding New York State and the Federal Government laws and regulations that govern confidentiality and the use and disclosure of our patients' protected health information (PHI), to include highly sensitive information related to HIV, mental health and substance abuse to include but not limited to protocols as set forth in:
    • Public Health Law 27F
    • NYS Department of Health Regulation Part 50-4
  • PHI includes any information that identifies an individual and that relates to the individual's health, treatment, or payment for that treatment.
  • I understand that Arnot Health policies forbid my accessing or disclosing PHI unless it is required to perform my job.
  • I understand that access or disclosure applies to information contained within the paper or electronic medical record, as well as information I become visually or verbally aware of in the course of work at Arnot Health.
  • I understand that if in the course of my employment I have access to highly sensitive confidential HIV-related information about patients, their spouse or partner, the confidentiality of this information is protected by law as referenced above and may not be disclosed without authorization and may not be released without a DOH approved release form.
  • Employees are not allowed to access their personal medical record or family members' medical records. Employees who wish to receive copies of their own medical record will contact the Medical Records Department (HIS) and/or set up a portal account.

In regards to the rules regarding access, disclosure or release of PHI, I acknowledge that:

  • Arnot Health has a "zero tolerance" policy on inappropriate access, disclosure, or release of PHI or any confidential patient information.
  • I understand that it is my responsibility to understand and follow federal and state law, as well as Arnot Health policies in regard to handling all confidential information (PHI), to include HIV related information, that I learn in the course of my employment at AH.
  • I understand that I may not post PHI on any social media website (Facebook, Twitter, etc.).
  • I understand that it is my responsibility to report any breach or suspected breach of PHI.
  • I am subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination for any violation of Arnot Health policy.
  • I may be held personally liable and subject to fines, criminal charges, and prosecution for a breach of confidentiality.
  • I understand that accessing patient records for personal knowledge or personal purposes is not tolerated.
  • I may not access my personal medical record or family member's medical record.
  • I understand that I am responsible for all access that occurs under my user ID.
  • I understand that these duties and standards apply even if I am no longer employed by Arnot Health.
  • Any questions related to access or disclosure of confidential patient records should be directed to the Arnot Health Privacy Officer or the Health Information Services (Medical Records) department.

I am aware Arnot Health does not permit any form of retaliation or intimidation regarding the reporting of a situation or concern. By signing this acknowledgement, I affirm I understand the Code of Conduct and my responsibility to report any concerns regarding violations of conduct or compliance.

The documents, policies related to these issues include:

  • HIPAA at Arnot Health (on the Intranet)
  • The Patient's Bill of Rights
  • Notice of Privacy Practices
  • Confidentiality Related to Medical Records
  • HIV confidentiality
  • Photographic and Videographic Consent
  • Compliance Reporting and Non-Retaliation
  • Password/Passphrase Policy
  • Uses & Disclosures of Patient Information
  • Authorization for Release of Information
  • Rules, Grounds For Dismissal
  • Social Media
